LIMITATIONS

Limitations

  • I

    Limited statistical power

    Sample size was too small to distinguish noise from true genetic signals among millions of variants. Findings represent hypothetical associations.

  • II

    Limited generalizability

    Cohorts were mostly European males. Results may not apply to all AAA patients.

  • III

    Potential artifacts

    The phenotype-extraction model struggled with difficult CT images, misclassifying similar-appearing lumen and thrombus. More training is needed for edge cases.

III

Potential artifacts

CONCLUSIONS

Take Home Messages

  • I

    Initial Genetic Map

    First map of genetic signals linked to disease-specific traits in AAA patients.

  • II

    Distinct Mechanisms

    Mechanisms in healthy individuals differ from those after disease onset.

  • III

    Trait-Specific Biology

    Each disease trait is driven by distinct biological pathways:

    • • Diameter: Vascular cell integrity
    • • Calcification: Inflammatory, vascular, and lipid metabolism
    • • Thrombus: Blood-cell organization and oxidative stress
